Gaurangi Kaushik (born 25 July 2002) is an actual child that is turning 18 this year. She is a recent high school graduate, and an aspiring dumbass. Kaushik, the daughter of Rajesh K. Sharma and Madhvi Sharma, was born in Vienna and raised across the globe. Her father is a diplomat and her mother is a housewife. She also has an elder sister (who is unarguably the best) named Gauri. As a teenager, she enjoyed gossiping with friends and borrowing her sister’s clothes and then never returning them. Her constant relocation due to her father’s profession never interfered with her popularity or her ability to bring joy regardless of every situation. Having been a skinny legend ™ since birth, and having two gorgeous dimples that she prides herself in she has never been afraid of the spotlight. Her hair is also incredibly voluminous and hair flip worthy. Any opportunity given to her has been tremendously fulfilled. She has received praise for her dancing, singing, athletics and fashion sense. She has won many accolades for her public speaking, and academics throughout her schooling. She also became a published author at the young age of 13. As her poem was sold as a part of Poetopia (2015 edition). The year of 2018 was a turning point for her, when she took commerce (with IP) as she hates mathematics. Her life took an uphill path after that, and she finally found a footing in her academics and social life. She is a Leo (its leo season baby!) , and hence her attributes seem to match her astrological sign to the tea. She is generous, compassionate, and protective of her loved ones. She has a charm that brings light to any dull moment. She is warm, enchanting and a delight to be around.
